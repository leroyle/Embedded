Embedded Developer Resources

This is a list of sites I've found useful. It's a work in progress. The sites are not necessarily in the order of my preference. Some fall into multiple catagories which may not be reflected below.

Blogs

  • Embedded.fm
    Elecia White, Christopher White

  • Embedded Artistry
    Phillip Johnston, Rozi Harri

  • EEVBlog
    David L. Jones Some time co-host of The Amp Hour podcase

  • Beningo Embedded Group
    Jacob Beningo

  • MCU on Eclipse
    Erich Styger's blog
    Lots of good info on getting various boards working with Eclipse, JTAG, debuggers, etc
    "This blog is because I have been asked to share my thoughts, tips and tricks around Eclipse, CodeWarrior, Kinetis Design Studio and anything else around software, microcontrollers and BBQ"
    "I am teaching as Professer embedded systems programming at the Lucerne University of Applied Sciences and Arts. Beside of that I’m working part time for NXP with helping out in the community forums or providing customer feedback on their products."

  • Hansel Minutes
    Scott Hanselman, he was big in the early Microsoft .Net world

  • SaltyTinkerer
    An article on bit manipulation in C

  • Interrupt
    Mohammad Afaneh

  • Jay Carlson
    Jay Carlson author of "The Amazing $1 microcontroller"

  • Sticky Bits – Powered by Feabhas
    Niall Cooling
    The dev has an article, An Introduction to Docker for Embedded Developers

  • The Ganssle Group
    Jack Ganssle He also has an e-newletter The Embedded Muse

  • Beningo Embedded Group
    Jacob Beningo

    DigiKey sponsored site supporting multiple instructors teaching various courses

    A pay per course elearning site.

    Some free, some not. Some will give you a certificate if you pay a nomimal fee
